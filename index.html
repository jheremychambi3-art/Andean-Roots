The Journey

A Clean and Traceable Production Process

From the rich Peruvian volcanic soil to the final shipment container, we ensure strict cold chain and hygiene protocols.

01

Sustainable Cultivation

Sown in the nutrient-rich, high-altitude Andean slopes of Peru. We prioritize ecological farming to yield intense rhizomes.

02

Hand-Harvest & Sorting

Each root is harvested by hand to prevent skin damage, and meticulously sorted into standardized sizes (Small, Medium, Premium).

03

Triple-Cleaning & Sanitizing

Thorough wash-cycle utilizing pressure jets and eco-friendly organic sanitizers. Air-dried carefully to remove moisture.

04

Reefer Container Logistics

Packed in boxes, stacked on clean pallets, and shipped in refrigerated Reefer containers maintained strictly at 12°C - 14°C.
